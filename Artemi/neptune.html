Neptune

Neptune is the eighth and farthest known planet from the Sun. It's the densest large planet, the third most massive planet, and the fourth largest planet in the Solar System by diameter. Its mass is 17 times that of Earth. While Neptune is smaller and denser than its neighbouring ice giant Uranus, it is significantly more massive. Since gases and liquids make up the majority of its composition. Despite some media depict it with a strong blue colour, its actual colour actually resembles closely to that of Uranus.
