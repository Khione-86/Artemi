Earth

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only astronomical object in our solar system known to harbor life currently. The fact that Earth is an ocean world—the only one in the Solar System that has liquid surface water—allows for this. The global ocean, which makes up 70.8% of the earth's crust, contains nearly all of the water on the planet. Land makes up the remaining 29.2% of the Earth's crust, and the majority of it is found in the land hemisphere as continental landmasses.
